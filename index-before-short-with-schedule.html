About The Conference

The First Israel UAE Intenational Annual Conference

The First Israel UAE Intenational Conference is an Annual international conference aimed to connect between Israel and the United Arab Emirates. The conference will be conducted 2 times each year, once in Israel and once in the UAE.

We invite you to register to our free pre-conference session.
